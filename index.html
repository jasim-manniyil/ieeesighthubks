Our Amazing Team

Who leeds IEEE Sight kerala section

" The IEEE SIGHT Kerala Section Team is a dedicated group of IEEE members, professionals, and volunteers who are committed to leveraging technology for humanitarian causes in the state of Kerala, India. The team consists of individuals with diverse backgrounds and expertise, including engineers, researchers, educators, and industry professionals, who share a common goal of using their skills to address societal challenges and improve the quality of life for underserved communities."
