Our recent Projects

STEM.py, a 16-hour IEEE Try Engineering funded Python Workshop Series, was organized by the IEEE SIGHT Kerala Section in collaboration with the IEEE Education Society and IEEE SB NIT Calicut.Included online sessions from May 27th,2023 and culminated in an offline session June 10th 2023.

The establishment of the IEEE SIGHT Kerala Section's first Research and Development (R&D) team marks a significant turning point in the organization's history. This remarkable accomplishment signals a conscious shift in the section's stance as a center for cutting-edge technological research and innovation. This team is ready to provide its members with specialized training in their chosen fields of interest thanks to the respected mentors leading the charge in diverse sectors. Utilizing the combined knowledge, high-impact humanitarian initiatives tackling pressing societal issues will be carried out. By doing this, the IEEE SIGHT R&D team not only hopes to offer novel solutions but also to encourage and develop a new generation of highly qualified engineers, ensuring a more promising future for both society and technology.

The IEEE SIGHT RandD Expo, held at the TKM College of Engineering in Kollam on September 17, 2023, was a landmark event that brought together distinguished guests, esteemed members of the IEEE community, and passionate enthusiasts of cutting-edge technology.

SIGHT Kerala Congress 2023 witnessed an array of insightful events aimed at inspiring and guiding the young minds of the engineering community towards innovative solutions with a humanitarian perspective. The event was organized by IEEE Kerala Section Sight, and it featured distinguished speakers who shared their expertise and experiences with the enthusiastic participants.Was filled with informative sessions and valuable insights.

Ideate for Humanity is an innovation cum ideation challenge organized by IEEE Kerala Section SIGHT as part of AKSC’23, conducted by IEEE LINK in TKM College of Engineering Kollam on 17th September 2023. The event persuaded the participants to collaborate and bring forth innovative solutions for real-world challenges. The event's theme was UN Sustainable Development Goals, particularly SDGs 10, 12, 13 and 14.

The main goal of the IEEE SIGHT Kerala Research and Development Team's humanitarian project "Resilient Light" is to provide dependable and long-lasting disaster management kits to individuals living in Kerala, India,'s flood-prone districts. These kits were carefully created to improve the communities' preparedness and resilience in the event of natural disasters, notably floods. Notably, the kits include power banks and solar-powered emergency lights, guaranteeing continuous access to vital services like illumination and communication during power outages brought on by disasters. Resilient Light seeks to strengthen and assist disadvantaged communities through this project so they can better withstand disasters and create a more secure and resilient future.

“Domestic & Drinking Reservoir with Renewable Energy- Bavelli (D2Re-B) funded project under IEEE SIGHT Kerala Section. The project mainly focuses on designing an energy efficient, economical water filtration unit in a tribal village of Bavelli in Wayanad District. The project was from 23/12/2022 to 25/12/2022.The finished project was inaugurated on 25 th of december 2022 at 12 PM by Mr. PV Balakrishnan, the Panchayat President of Thirunelli.
